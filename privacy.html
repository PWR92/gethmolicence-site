Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Get HMO Licence (gethmolicence.co.uk) is operated by Patrick Roper ("we", "us"). We provide document preparation and application-handling services for HMO licence applications in England. For any privacy question or request, contact us via the enquiry form on the homepage.

What we collect and why

When you submit our enquiry form we collect: your name, phone number, property postcode, occupancy details, optionally your email address, and a technical field recording how you found us (for example, which advert or search keyword). We use this to respond to your enquiry, check your council's licensing requirements, and provide a quote. Our lawful basis is legitimate interests (responding to an enquiry you initiated). If you instruct us, we collect further information needed to prepare your application (property details, certificates), on the lawful basis of performing our contract with you.

Who processes it

  • FormSubmit relays your form submission to our email; submissions may transit servers outside the UK (including the US).
  • Netlify hosts this website and processes standard server logs.
  • Google Ads may set cookies if you arrived via an advert; we do not run additional tracking or analytics tags on this site.
  • Microsoft (Outlook/Hotmail) stores enquiry emails in our mailbox.

Retention

Enquiries that don't become clients are deleted within 12 months. Client records are kept for 6 years after the engagement ends, in line with professional record-keeping requirements.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, object to or restrict our processing, and request portability. Contact us via the form to exercise any of these. You can also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data, send marketing emails you haven't asked for, or share your details with anyone except as needed to prepare your application (for example, a floor-plan provider, with your knowledge).
